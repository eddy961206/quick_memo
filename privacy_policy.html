Privacy Policy for Simplist Memo Of All Time (SMOAT)

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Memo Data: The notes you create and save using the extension.
  • Theme Preference: Your selected theme (light or dark) for the extension.
  • Auto-Save Preference: Your preference for enabling or disabling auto-save functionality.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and maintain the functionality of the extension.
  • Improve user experience by remembering your preferences.
  • Ensure that your notes are saved automatically if you choose that option.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. We do not share your data with third parties.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
